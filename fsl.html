The Brief

Abbott Diabetes Care are launching a new initiative, which is set to launch September 1st 2014. eMedFusion won the RFP to design and build the web presemce for this new product.

Abbott wanted this site to be resonsive site to advertise their product, and help market it in this highly competative and innovative market. This site had to be content managable, and connect with a veriety of different services.

My role was as Lead UX and Lead Developer on the project, and involved liaising with the client, architecting the system, writing the functional specification and SOW, and managing the development day to day.

Key Responsiblities

  • System architecture
  • Wireframes
  • Functional specification
  • Managing day to day dev
    • Hiring freelance developers
    • Managing version control

In this role I attempted to introduce eMedFusion to numerous tools and techniques including SCRUM, Git, Grunt, Jenkins, UX, and a UX focused methodology.

Technologoy I used

  • Yeoman
  • Grunt
  • Drupal
  • HTML/CSS (IE8)
  • elementQuery
